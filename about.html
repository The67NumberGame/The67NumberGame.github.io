What Is The 67 Number Game?

Every day, four numbers are revealed. Your challenge is to combine them using maths — addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, powers, factorials, and parentheses — to reach the target of 67. Every number must be used exactly once.

It's a game that rewards creative thinking, number intuition, and a healthy dose of stubbornness. Whether you crack it in seconds or spend your lunch break puzzling over it, that's exactly the idea.
